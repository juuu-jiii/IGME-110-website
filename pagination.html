PAGINATION

Pagination has been on the web for ages. It is such a commonality among websites on the Internet that it has caused many to believe that pagination is an age-old practice, that it is the "correct" way to format web pages [9].

Contrary to popular belief, however, websites were not always paginated, and they certainly did not start out that way. Rather, a scrolling layout was adopted at the very outset, with paginated layouts appearing much later [9].

Pagination, as an implementation, has been quite controversial among fans of the Internet. As you probably can imagine, it has its benefits, but those do not come without a hefty price tag attached to them. With that being said, I have created lists of said benefits and drawbacks.
