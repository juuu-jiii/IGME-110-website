#1: FRICTION

"In user experience, friction is defined as interactions that inhibit people from intuitively and painlessly achieving their goals within a digital interface." -Victoria Young [12]

Repeated clicking to get to a block of desired information is all but intuitive. In most cases, friction ensues, which, in turn, leads to frustration [10].
