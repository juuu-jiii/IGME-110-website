PAGINATION: DRAWBACKS

#2: INTRUSIVE ADVERTISEMENTS

The general consensus is that advertisements online are becoming more and more intrusive by the day. The most frequently-heard complaint is that such advertisements make webpage visitors feel as though they feel watched [8].

Pagination amplifies this somewhat, by providing a place for such advertisements to reside, ready to pounce on the unsuspecting visitor. Too many advertisements also slows down webpage loading times.
