PAGINATION: BENEFITS

#2: INCREASED ADVERTISEMENT REVENUE

More web pages is equivalent to more advertising opportunities. It is a mathematical relationship best described to be "directly proportional". Splitting an article into 2 pages means twice as many advertisements and double the income, and the list goes on.

Temptation evetually takes over, and turns into regular practice. Articles which would have little to no trouble fitting in one page are, as a result, broken up unnecessarily. [9]
