Szun Kidd Choi IGME110 - 04 Video Exercise

I made a makeshift Mini Cooper commercial, because why not? After all, I did unintentionally stumble upon 5-6 videos of the same MINI on Pexels just conveniently laid out side-by-side. Why put such a great opportunity to waste? I also tried to be as minimalistic with the design as possible, by selecting a font that was - to me, at least - simple, yet sophisticated enough to look professional.

The music was also randomly selected because I thought its upbeat nature fit the overall setting of the video. How it somehow manages to sync with the video in certain parts is beyond me. But hey, you know what they say: never look a gift horse in the mouth, so I shan't. I will say, however, that I'm still amused over it all.
