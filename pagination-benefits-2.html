PAGINATION: BENEFITS

#1: SEARCH-ENGINE OPTIMISATION

Search-engine optimisation (abbreviated SEO) is a technique whose main goal is to attract as many visitors to a website as possible. It entails ensuring the website in question is one of the first to appear on the list of search results displayed by a search engine. Pagination is one of the ways to achieve this.
