HOW HAVE ADVERTISEMENTS AFFECTED MEDIA TODAY?

SZUN KIDD CHOI, ROCHESTER INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

As an extension to my research paper, I have put together this website to show the effects advertisements have had on the media we consume on a daily basis, in ways words alone cannot provide.

All in all, my goal here is not to persuade you, dear visitor, about what I make of these effects it is merely to document the severity of what I believe to be a predicament. In the end, it is ultimately up to you to form your own conclusions regarding such matters, and to decide whether reformation is needed sometime in the near future.
